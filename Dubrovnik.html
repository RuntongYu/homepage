Dubrovnik Croatia

Often referred to as “The Pearl of the Adriatic”, Dubrovnik is a charming city in southern Croatia.
Begin the day drinking and cliff jumping at Buza, a bar situated along rocks on the city wall overlooking the Adriatic, and end the day by watching the magnificent sunset over the sea.
No matter where you are in the city, there is always a beach nearby.
