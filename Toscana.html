Tuscany Italy

Tuscany is famous for its bucolic scenery. In Pienza, Siena's town, the hillsides are beautiful and the wheat fields nearby, blown by summer winds, are spectacular.
Rigg Farm is a square structure in the distance, especially in the morning fog effect is very beautiful.
Rigo Farm is a typical representative of the Valley of Val d'Orcia, which can be shot from all angles on its nearby slopes, with different effects.
